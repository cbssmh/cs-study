1. Title
The State of Global AI Diffusion in 2026

2. Source
- Author / Organization: Juan Lavista Ferres, Microsoft
- Link: Not provided
- Date: May 7, 2026

3. One-line Summary
Global AI adoption continued rising in early 2026, with faster diffusion in Asia, a widening North-South adoption gap, and strong growth in AI-assisted software development.

4. Key Points
- Global AI usage rose from 16.3% to 17.8% of the working-age population in Q1 2026.
- 26 economies now exceed 30% AI usage among working-age people.
- The UAE remains the global leader in AI diffusion at 70.1%.
- The United States rose from 24th to 21st place, with a 31.3% usage rate.
- AI adoption accelerated in Asia, especially in South Korea, Thailand, and Japan.
- Improved AI capabilities in Asian languages contributed to adoption growth.
- The AI adoption gap widened between the Global North and Global South.
- Usage reached 27.5% in the Global North versus 15.4% in the Global South.
- Microsoft measures AI diffusion using anonymized telemetry adjusted for device share, internet penetration, and population.
- AI coding tools contributed to a major increase in software production, with global git pushes up 78% year over year.
- U.S. software developer employment reached about 2.2 million in 2025, up 8.5% year over year.
- Early 2026 data shows U.S. software developer employment in March was about 4% higher than in March 2025.
